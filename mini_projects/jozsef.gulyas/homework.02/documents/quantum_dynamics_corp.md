# Quantum Dynamics Corporation - Company Overview

## Company History

Quantum Dynamics Corporation (QDC) was founded on **March 15, 2019** by Dr. Elena Vasquez and Marcus Chen in Portland, Maine. The company specializes in quantum-resistant encryption algorithms for enterprise cloud storage solutions.

## Key Milestones

- **2019**: Company founded with initial seed funding of $3.2 million
- **2020**: Released QDEncrypt 1.0 on July 22, 2020
- **2021**: Acquired smaller competitor "CryptoShield Systems" for $47 million on November 8, 2021
- **2022**: Opened European headquarters in Lyon, France on February 14, 2022
- **2023**: Reached 10,000 enterprise customers worldwide on September 3, 2023
- **2024**: IPO on NASDAQ with ticker symbol QDYC on April 18, 2024, opening at $67 per share

## Products and Services

### QDEncrypt Suite
The flagship product line includes:
- **QDEncrypt Pro**: Released June 2020, supports up to 2048-qubit encryption
- **QDEncrypt Enterprise**: Released January 2022, includes AI-powered threat detection
- **QDEncrypt Cloud**: Released August 2023, fully managed cloud encryption service

## Research and Development

QDC maintains three research facilities:
1. **Portland Innovation Lab** - Main R&D center, 240 employees
2. **Lyon Advanced Cryptography Center** - European research hub, 95 employees
3. **Singapore Quantum Research Institute** - Opened December 1, 2023, 63 employees

The company invests 28% of annual revenue into R&D activities.

## Recent Developments

On **October 7, 2024**, QDC announced a strategic partnership with the International Cybersecurity Alliance to develop open-source quantum-resistant protocols. The initiative, called "Project Sentinel," aims to release the first standardized protocol by **June 2025**.

The company also broke ground on a new headquarters building in Portland on **August 29, 2024**. The 450,000 square-foot facility will accommodate up to 2,000 employees and is expected to be completed by **March 2026**.
